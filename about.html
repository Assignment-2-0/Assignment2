About Us

The Big 5 is a technology company that sells clients low cost, high quality electronic devices. The Big 5 is based in Malaysia and has more than 300 employees. The Big 5 was established in 2007. Over the years, The Big 5 have been growing their businesses around the world by collaborating with large corporations to advertise their products overseas. Their products consist mainly of laptops, keyboards, tablets and other more. The Big 5 is headquartered in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Other than that, there are two other branches which are in TTDI and SS7. As a result of the influence of online shopping, they have decided to transform their operating system from physical shops to online to boost up more popularity.
